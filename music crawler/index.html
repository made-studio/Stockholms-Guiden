US Top 100:

Some observerations can however be made about each respective country. In the US rap and hip-hop music has a very strong dominance while R&B and Country also is quite popular. There is also latino/urban-latino and americana songs, genres that can not be found in Sweden or the UK.

Sweden Top 100:

In Sweden Pop music was really popular, much more so than in the US and the UK. There were no genres exclusive to Sweden that really stod out, only genres like childrens and holiday music that do not tell us anything about Swedens taste for music. Overall Swedens music taste was quite similar to the UK's.

UK Top 100:

Pop music and rap/hip-hop was also beloved genres in the UK but dance music and rock was also very common. Electronic music and Afrobeat was also unique genres for the UK. The popularity of dance, electronic and house music could be a reflection of the UK's love for clubbing.
